Trika Initiatives

Fish Bin

Every man, every woman, every inhabitant must contemplate the day when it no longer be inhabitable. Such high is the level of pollution on this planet.The alarmingly increased presence of plastics around us is surely a threat to Mother Earth. Thus, proper disposal of plastics gain significance. For the proper disposal of plastic wastes, its proper collection from public places is important. As a prior step, fish bins, which are permanent installations resembling shapes of fishes, for the collection of plastic wastes, will be constructed at places of frequent public gatherings like beaches. This has proved to attract and encourage people, especially children in collecting litered plastic waste. STEPS is on this venture to remind people that 'better now or never' is the plight of our planet and we ought to spend a great deal of time with the nature.

Divyang

Easy accessibility to public places is still a dream to physically disabled in our country. Being dusted as the disabled is one of the hardest part of life. As a preliminary contribution from our part, in transforming our city into disabled – friendly one, some of the common public spots in Kollam district is made accessible by constructing supporting infrastructures and easy transportation of disabled ones is achieved. Supporting infrastructures includes ramps for easy movement of wheelchairs, tactile-tiled floors for assisting visually impaired etc. Let us provide wings to their tied dreams.

Mathrusanthwanam

Being socially active can be as beneficial as being physically active. Keeping this in mind, as a part of Trika'20, we had been up with the venture 'Mathrusanthwanam'. Mathrusanthwanam was an initiative to visit the old age homes every months and engage those pure souls into a world of colour and joy. A merry-full gathering of our students, helped them stay emotionally strong and makes them feel connected to the world around them. As a first step, a visit to Injuvila Old Age Home was held on 2nd February 2020. Holding up those wrinkled hands, sitting with the tired hearts and pacifying them, 'Mathrusanthwanam' surely rejuvenated those innocent souls.

Waterbody Cleaning

Waterbodies are in fact the lifelines of a society and heart of civilization. But with rapid urbanization and unplanned development, we are witnessing dying lakes and rivers day-by-day. Both quality and quantity of water is becoming questionable. The examples from all around the world shows that, cleaning polluted water bodies are difficult, but possible. ‘Drop by drop, the ocean is filled’- Keeping this proverb in mind, as a beginning step, cleaning of a part of selected waterbody in Kollam district is planned, hoping other governmental and non-governmental organization will be influenced and continue this venture resulting in cleaner waterbodies.

Scholarship Exam

“Dreams become regrets when left in the mind”. Talented young minds are not able to achieve their dreams just because of lack of availability of resources for them. Hence to motivate them, to guide them in the path of achieving their goals, STEPS was up with a venture. A scholarship exam was planned for school students and the scholarship amount received could be utilized by them to receive appropriate coaching to achieve their dream course. The students in the top 5 positions were awarded a scholarship amount of Rs 5000 which could certainly add a lot to their path of achieving success
