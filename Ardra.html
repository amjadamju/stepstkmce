Aardra-2015-17

Aardra-15 – a state wide stem cell donor registry drive organized in July – August 2015. The number of registrations shot up by 4.2% in India and by 18.5 % in Kerala. It was a huge success, 4232 registrations with just 15 days.282 REGISTRATIONS PER DAY.3/4th of TKMCE students has volunteered this event. District and college coordinators spend their time and energy for the successful completion of each camps.

Aardra 16- Successfully conducted "AARDRA 2016" the biggest blood stem cell donor registration drive which happened in Kerala where over 6500 people came forward to register as donors for the noble cause of saving lives. AARDRA was conducted with the help of STEPS- a social student wing of TKM College of Engineering, Kollam and ASTER MIMS Hospital. This was a 2-month program which included awareness and registration camps in all districts with 21 colleges joining for the cause.
