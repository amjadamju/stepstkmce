Aardra-2015-17

Aardra, where hearts melt, souls become divine! Aardra, the Stem Cell Donation Camp organised by STEPS TKMCE witnessed a large flock of people agreeing to donate their stem cells to the needy. This initiative of STEPS urged people to know about the depths of pain that people suffering from diseases like luekemia, lymphoma etc has. This legacy being dated back to the year 2014, has now crossed it's boundaries, being a word of hope to many.
