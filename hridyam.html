Hridyam

Hearts to pacify each other, where pain and sorrow shared and cured. Pacifying each other, supporting and raising side by side, every souls conquered our hearts. Students of TKMCE truly up with venture of empowering people and society with Hridyam. The gathering of the pain and palliative was organised by STEPS at Adventure Park, Kollam where the inmates of Mundakkal Old Age Home and pain and palliative patients were brought together. Tasty food, mesmerising music and happy faces were the beauty of that evening.
