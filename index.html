About Us

Promote the use of technology to solve social problems and encouraging engineering students to solve engineering problems of social importance.Promoting social entrepreneurship and social organizations.Encourage, support and inspire young people to join/support various NGOs working in the field of social entrepreneurship.Ensuring Inclusive and Equitable Quality Education and Promoting Lifelong Learning Opportunities for All.Spreading the message of love, kindness and empathy. Engendering social responsibility and compassion to the fellow beings, in the generations to come.

Events

First Love

Cherishing the shattered hearts and lives with smiles and joy, for all intents and purposes is heavenly.When the will, the benevolence, and the social conscience of every TKMCEian roused itself, it created a memorable evening colored with the bright hues of love and care. And we called those hours of sharing, caring and expressing, "FIRST LOVE". Our Valentines were the inmates of Mundayckal and Inchuvila Old Age Homes

Love Locks

25 Girls came forward, with heads held high, hair too long and exceptional confidence to donate their hair strands to the cancer patients.The campus witnessed a great evening that captured the vibes of a girl's love for hair and no less her love for society.

Sukrita

A social welfare initiative using technology  to assist and empower the differently abled .As the after work of Sukrita 17, we were able to gift the following to the most disadvantaged families: 1. Air bed 2. Sewing Machine 3. 2 wheelchairs

Back to School

STEPS distributed school kits to the most deserving 79 school students in our locality. Each school kit worth Rs.700 contains School bag,notebooks,umbrella and stationary. An amount of Rs.52000 has been spent from STEPS account for this purpose.

Ardra

Aardra-15 – a state wide stem cell donor registry drive organized in July – August 2015. The number of registrations shot up by 4.2%%in India and by 18.5%%in Kerala. It was a huge success, 4232 registrations with just 15 days.282 REGISTRATIONS PER DAY.3/4th of TKMCE students has volunteered this event. District and college coordinators spend their time and energy for the successful completion of each camps.

Hridyam

Hridyam,a cultural gathering of pain and palliative patients, was held at Adventure Park, Kollam.The Inmates of mundakkal oldage home and pain and palliative patients were brought there, where their tired minds and souls get rejuvenated. The TKMCE Music Club entertained them with their mesmerizing music.
