About Us

We are a student-run organization from TKM college, committed to serve the society and the needy. STEPS always considers human values and understands the need for a society where education and basic freedom is available to all. We, at STEPS, work towards making each day of a needy person worthwhile. We are committed to providing motivation towards education, health and other well-being.

FIRST LOVE

Cherishing the shattered hearts and lives with smiles and joy, for all intents and purposes is heavenly.When the will, the benevolence, and the social conscience of every TKMCEian roused itself, it created a memorable evening colored with the bright hues of love and care. And we called those hours of sharing, caring and expressing,"FIRST LOVE". Our Valentines were the inmates of Mundayckal and Inchuvila Old Age Homes

LOVELOCKS

25 Girls came forward, with heads held high, hair too long and exceptional confidence to donate their hair strands to the cancer patients.The campus witnessed a great evening that captured the vibes of a girl's love for hair and no less her love for society.

HRIDYAM

Hridyam,a cultural gathering of pain and palliative patients, was held at Adventure Park, Kollam.The Inmates of mundakkal oldage home and pain and palliative patients were brought there, where their tired minds and souls get rejuvenated. The TKMCE Music Club entertained them with their mesmerizing music.
