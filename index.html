About Us

STEPS Students of TKMCE for the Empowerment of People and Society, abbreviated as STEPS today stands as the epitome of gentleness under the minarets of TKM. The platform where every engineering mind turns humanitarian. The place that rejuvenates the minds of many, reforms the society and revives humanity. Through these years STEPS had devised a new definition for humanity where spreading smiles where the ultimate goal. In this world of melting values, STEPS acts as a torchbearer holding values high. Holding the wrinkled fingers, pacifying the broken hearts and helping them catch their shelled dreams, students of TKMCE have empowered the society a lot of people and by being by their side as they take each step forward. This is a platform where engineers use their heart and brain together for empowering the society. A space where technology could solves many issues. This was what made STEPS different.

Trika'20

Trika' 20 was an initiative from STEPS that aims in empowering and assisting various sectors of the society that need assistance for empowering themselves, by creating opportunities that could have long lasting impacts on their life.
