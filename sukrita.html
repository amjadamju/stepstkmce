Sukrita '17

Sukrita which means 'good act'. Sukrita witnessed the good acts of the best souls of TKMCE. Sukrita brought together children from various backgrounds with many shelled talents to open up and showcase Infront of the audience. Breaking the shackles, removing the veils, many singers, dancers, painters, narrators, authors, scientists and many more came out. Giving them the appropriate guidance, volunteers of STEPS showed their brilliant act of rejuvenating those innocent hearts. The love and care that they showed were gifted back with mind robbing smiles. Adding the term 'satisfaction' to the equation of happiness, Sukrita truly revived humanity and reformed the society.
