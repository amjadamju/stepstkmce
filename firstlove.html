First Love

February 14, Valentine's Day celebrated in the most splendid manner at TKMCE by STEPS and College Union. Returning to the first love we all had, our parents, who pampered us, sang us lullabies, narrated bedtime stories, chanted us rhymes and even more. The evening of the day at the college became soothing with the overflow of happiness from the hearts of the parents as well as the students. Rescheduling the normal routines of Valentine's day, this time the minarets served the best feasts ever. Roses not to your valentine but to those wrinkled hands, songs not romantic but pampering, gifts not to your better half but to the best ones you own. This was how the minarets celebrated this Valentine's Day. Bowing to the most beautiful love in this world, the evening became most divine.
